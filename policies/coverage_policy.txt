DIABETES CARE COVERAGE POLICY - PLAN B

Insulin and diabetes medication: Insulin (all types) is covered at 80% under Plan B
after the annual deductible of $500 is met. Oral diabetes medications such as
metformin are covered at 90% with no deductible. Continuous glucose monitors (CGMs)
are covered only with prior authorization from the member's physician.

Preventive care: Annual diabetes screening (HbA1c test) is covered at 100% with no
deductible, once per calendar year. Additional HbA1c tests require a physician order
and are covered at 80%.

Specialist visits: Endocrinologist visits are covered at 70% after deductible.
A referral from a primary care physician is required. Without a referral, the visit
is not covered and the member pays the full cost.

Exclusions: Weight-loss programs, gym memberships, and over-the-counter supplements
are not covered under Plan B, even when recommended by a physician.
