CLAIMS SUBMISSION AND PROCESSING RULES

Submission deadline: All claims must be submitted within 90 days of the date of
service. Claims submitted after 90 days are automatically denied, unless the
provider documents a valid delay reason (system outage, member hospitalization).

Required fields: Every claim must include the member ID, provider NPI number,
diagnosis code (ICD-10), procedure code (CPT), date of service, and billed amount.
Claims missing any required field are rejected and returned to the provider,
not denied. Rejected claims can be corrected and resubmitted without penalty.

Processing time: Clean claims are processed within 15 business days. Claims
flagged for manual review may take up to 30 business days. Members can check
claim status online or by calling member services.

Duplicate claims: A claim with the same member, provider, procedure code, and
date of service as an existing claim is flagged as a duplicate and denied.
Providers should not resubmit a claim while the original is still processing.
